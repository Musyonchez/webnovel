SCHOOLS OF KNOWLEDGE - Dominion of the Wizarding World

CORE SCHOOLS (The Seven Great Schools):

1. The Body Forge – mastery of flesh, bone, and transformation, producing living weapons
2. The Venom Archive – plague, poison, and decay, turning corruption into a weapon
3. The Hive Codex – insect swarms, parasites, and hive-mind control
4. The Elemental Concord – fire, storm, earth, and tide bound into destructive forms
5. The Astral Lexicon – stars, fate, illusion, and mind-binding arts
6. The Necrotome – death, shadow, blood, and communion with the dead
7. The Aether Crucible – pure arcane force, sigils, and enchantment

EXPANDED SCHOOLS:

8. The Green Veil – botany, herbalism, toxic blooms, and sentient plants
9. The Elixirium – potion-making, alchemy, and transmutations brewed in liquid form
10. The Forge of Relics – artifact creation, rune-crafting, and binding powers into tools
11. The Bestiary Codex – taming, breeding, and fusing beasts into magical companions
12. The Chronomerium – time fragments, memory, and causality distortions
13. The Void Archive – portals, space manipulation, and void-energy weaponization
14. The Soul Codex – contracts, soulbinding, and manipulation of essence

APPRENTICE CURRICULUM (Rank 1 Entry-Level Classes):

CORE FOUNDATION (Everyone must take):
- Mana Theory I – basics of channeling, circulation, and safety
- Spell Engraving – crafting and stabilizing your first Rank 1 spell-circle
- Potioncraft Basics – every wizard learns to brew minor potions for survival
- Arcane Script & Runes – the written language of magic, foundation for artifacts
- World Beasts & Ecology – identifying magical creatures, plants, and hazards

SPECIALIZATION ELECTIVES (Choose a path):
- Fleshcrafting I – study of muscles, organs, and blood, early intro to Body Forge
- Viridarium – toxic herbs, healing flora, and the dangers of magical botany
- Venom Studies – poisons, diseases, antidotes → seed of the Venom Archive
- Insectarium – training small familiars (spiders, beetles, wasps) → Hive Codex track
- Elemental Primer – shaping sparks, drops, and pebbles → Elemental Concord
- Astral Sight – meditation on stars and illusions → Astral Lexicon track
- Shadows & Ash – corpse preparation, basic undead → Necrotome entry
- Enchanting Workshop I – imbuing stones, talismans → Forge of Relics
- Elixir-Brewing I – faster potion branch → Elixirium deep path
- Void Glyphs – baby steps in space-folding, dangerous but high-prestige
- Time Fragments I – learning memory crystals and "rewind" spells → Chronomerium
- Soul Whispers – binding tiny spirits to candles or charms → Soul Codex seed

FORBIDDEN/"GRAY" CLASSES (Only some schools allow):
- Blood Arts – using your own blood as catalyst
- Pestilence Lab – deliberate cultivation of plagues
- Parasite Studies – worms, leeches, and body-hijacking magic

MC'S CHOSEN PATH:
- Warlock Wizard (Chimera bonding)
- Insect Tamer (Hive control)
- External focus on poison/disease delivery through insects
