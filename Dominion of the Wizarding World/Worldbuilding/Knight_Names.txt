KNIGHT NAMES - Dominion of the Wizarding World

MAIN CHARACTER:
Morvain Malaroth (House Malaroth)

NOBLE HOUSE VEYLOR (20% - Same Surname):
- Edric Veylor – stern eldest son, proud, looks down on lesser knights
- Cador Veylor – hot-headed cousin, obsessed with duels
- Althar Veylor – studious, knight-scholar type, rare among their house
- Rhovan Veylor – ambitious, tries to cozy up to wizards

NOBLE HOUSE DORNATH (Militaristic):
- Varik Dornath – grizzled veteran, strict commander type
- Kaelen Dornath – brash duelist, obsessed with glory
- Serina Dornath – rare female knight, tactically brilliant, underestimated

NOBLE HOUSE HALDRAKE (Wealthy Merchants-turned-nobles):
- Osric Haldrake – silver-tongued, values coin over honor
- Maeron Haldrake – secretly trains in forbidden techniques for power
- Elyra Haldrake – ambitious daughter, sharp wit, often mocks knights as "iron fools"

NOBLE HOUSE CALVORN (Borderland Beast-hunters):
- Darius Calvorn – famous beast-slayer, pragmatic and ruthless
- Thyren Calvorn – reckless thrill-seeker, charges headlong into danger
- Lyssia Calvorn – calculating and cold, believes knights should evolve or perish

HOUSE MALAROTH INFO:
- Motto: "From rot we rise, from rot we reign" / "Decay is truth"
- Crest: Mirrored black M (like mandibles or fangs)
- Colors: Black + sickly green + tarnished silver
- Reputation: Cursed but resilient, resistant to disease and toxins
- Known for surviving plagues, wars of attrition
- Among wizards: bloodline "tainted" with resistance, perfect for disease/poison schools
