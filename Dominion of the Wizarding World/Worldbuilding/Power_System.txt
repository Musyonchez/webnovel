POWER SYSTEM - Dominion of the Wizarding World

WIZARD RANKS & PROGRESSION:

APPRENTICE RANKS:
- Apprentice Rank 1: Basic mana sensing, crude spells
- Apprentice Rank 2: Better mana control, spell models
- Apprentice Rank 3: Stable core, ready for advancement

WIZARD RANKS:
- Rank 1 Wizard: First engraved spell (minimal mana cost, permanent access)
- Rank 2 Wizard: Second engraved spell
- Rank 3 Wizard: Third engraved spell
- Rank 4 Wizard: First Law comprehension (reality manipulation begins)
- Rank 5 Wizard: Deeper Law resonance
- Rank 6 Wizard: Combined Laws
- Rank 7 Wizard: Domain manifestation (portable reality)
- Rank 8 Wizard: Domain sovereignty (anchored in reality)
- Rank 9 Wizard: Domain world (planet-sized domains)

KNIGHT RANKS:
- Apprentice Knight: Learning breathing, muscle control
- Low Knight: Enhanced reflexes, fast healing, burst power
- Intermediate Knight: Faster than arrows, limited aura armor
- High Knight: Superhuman endurance, body hardens like steel
- Legendary Knight: Rare, aura body can match magic briefly

POWER EQUIVALENCIES:
- Low Knight = Wizard Apprentice Rank 1
- Intermediate Knight = Wizard Apprentice Rank 2
- High Knight = Wizard Apprentice Rank 3
- Legendary Knight = Rank 1 Wizard (hard fight)

ENGRAVED SPELL PROGRESSION:
- Rank 1: Defense (shield, barrier, armor spell)
- Rank 2: Offense (elemental strike, summoning weapon, destructive spell)
- Rank 3: Utility/Assistance (mind shield, poison mist, healing burst, stealth)

LAWS (Rank 4+ Understanding):
Fire, Ice, Gravity, Life, Death, Time, Space, Cause/Effect, Decay, Pestilence, Bond/Control, etc.

DOMAINS (Rank 7+ Manifestation):
- Rank 7: Domain manifestation (personal reality)
- Rank 8: Domain sovereignty (anchored reality)
- Rank 9: Domain world (living reality)

MANA MECHANICS:
- Wizards = living mana batteries
- Mana capacity increases with rank
- Mana regeneration depends on environment
- Mana-rich areas = passive regen, efficient casting
- Barren zones = no regen, exhaustion

MANA RECOVERY:
- Mana Stones: Slow absorption, good for cultivation
- Mana Potions: Instant absorption, emergency use, can cause addiction/burnout

WIZARD RADIATION:
- Absorbing mana makes wizards radioactive
- Higher ranks = more lethal radiation
- Kills normal humans, mutates plants/animals
- Rank 7-9 can't stay on planet without devastating regions
