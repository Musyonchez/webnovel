STORY ARCS - Dominion of the Wizarding World

ARC 1: TRANSMIGRATION & KNIGHT TRAINING (Chapters 1-20)

PURPOSE: Introduce MC, establish knight culture, foreshadow wizarding world

CHAPTER 1 DETAILED BREAKDOWN:

BACKGROUND SETUP:
- MC had AI chip from previous world (scientific breakthrough, everyone had one)
- Chip embedded in head, fused with soul during transmigration
- Original Morvain died from brain injury (assassin's attack - kidnapping attempt)
- Assassin killed by father, no identifiable traces
- Father is Marquis of Kingdom of Ashenwald, High Knight rank
- Two senior knights under father (both Intermediate Knights)

CHAPTER 1 SEQUENCE:
1. MC wakes up, chip activates, gets memories back when seeing servant girl
2. Servant girl brings bath
3. MC goes to courtyard, sees knights training
4. Chip scans training: [SCANNING] - finds advanced body exercises with "something extra"
5. MC interested, asks chip to record
6. Sees father walking with senior knight - both look big and powerful
7. Chip scans them: the "something small" in regular knights is much larger in them
8. Father notices MC, motions senior knight away, approaches
9. Father asks if brain injury has healed, MC says he's fine
10. MC continues watching training, shows interest (father surprised - injury changed him)
11. Father asks if MC wants to learn, MC nods
12. Father takes MC to private yard with other senior knight
13. Senior knight greets both, father asks him to show MC basics, then leaves
14. Knight demonstrates stance, MC tries alone - finds it difficult
15. MC asks chip to scan knight and correct his posture
16. Knight watches MC go from sloppy to almost perfect in real time
17. Knight changes stance to test MC - same result (sloppy to near-perfect)
18. After 4th stance, MC collapses from exhaustion
19. Knight calls servants for food, returns to own training
20. MC asks chip to record knight while too tired to think
21. MC eats, recovers, restarts training from first stance
22. Pattern: push to extreme → eat → rest → restart, repeats all day
23. Same training cycle continues for 3 days
24. Each day shows small improvements in endurance and form
25. Knight becomes intrigued by MC's learning speed and determination
26. Chapter ends with MC having established foundation routine

REMAINING ARC 1 BEATS:
- Chapters 2-4: Knight training progression, AI optimization, family dynamics
- Chapters 5-7: First combat, shows cold efficiency vs knight honor
- Chapters 8-10: Discovery of mana potential
- Chapters 11-13: Knight duel/trial, wins via cunning
- Chapters 14-16: Mana aptitude test reveals wizard talent
- Chapters 17-20: Family torn, departure preparations, foreshadowing
