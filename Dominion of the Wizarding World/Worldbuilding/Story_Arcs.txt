STORY ARCS - Dominion of the Wizarding World

ARC 1: TRANSMIGRATION & KNIGHT TRAINING (Chapters 1-20)

PURPOSE: Introduce MC, establish knight culture, foreshadow wizarding world

KEY BEATS:
- Chapter 1: MC transmigrates into Morvain Malaroth, AI chip activates
- Chapters 2-4: Knight training, family dynamics, AI optimization
- Chapters 5-7: First combat, shows cold efficiency vs knight honor
- Chapters 8-10: Discovery of mana potential
- Chapters 11-13: Knight duel/trial, wins via cunning
- Chapters 14-16: Mana aptitude test reveals wizard talent
- Chapters 17-20: Family torn, departure preparations, foreshadowing

ARC 2: JOURNEY TO WIZARD SCHOOL & SELECTION (Chapters 21-50)

PURPOSE: Contrast knight vs wizard worlds, introduce wizard brutality, MC chooses dark path

KEY BEATS:
- Chapters 21-23: Journey across continents, cultural differences
- Chapters 24-27: First encounter with wizard power, reality check
- Chapters 28-30: Dangers on road, MC uses knight training to survive
- Chapters 31-35: Arrival at wizard city, alien environment
- Chapters 36-42: School selection trials, tests of cruelty and wit
- Chapters 43-47: MC chooses Warlock path despite warnings
- Chapters 48-50: Initiation ritual, becomes Rank 1 Apprentice
