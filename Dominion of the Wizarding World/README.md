# Dominion of the Wizarding World

## Project Overview
Web novel about Morvain Malaroth, a transmigrator with an AI chip who becomes a powerful wizard in a brutal magical world.

## Main Character
- **Name:** Morvain Malaroth (House Malaroth)
- **Background:** Transmigrated from modern world with AI chip fused to soul
- **Current Status:** 11-year-old noble's son learning knight training
- **Future Path:** Knight training → Wizard discovery → Dark School → Warlock path (Chimera bonding) + Insect Tamer

## World Setting
- **Kingdom:** Ashenwald (knight-dominated continent)
- **Magic System:** Knights (body cultivation) vs Wizards (mana + laws + domains)
- **Tone:** Dark, brutal, survival of the fittest
- **Key Concept:** Knights are "failed experiments" created by wizards

## Story Arc Progress
- **Arc 1:** Transmigration & Knight Training (Chapters 1-20) - IN PROGRESS
- **Arc 2:** Journey to Wizard School & Selection (Chapters 21-50)
- **Future:** Warlock path, school founding, void campaigns, transcendence
